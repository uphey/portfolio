Anybooks Farm
Podcast Videos

"Anybooks Farm" is a Cantonese educational podcast channel dedicated to simplifying challenging reads. I elevate the listening experience with engaging audio mixing and video editing techniques.

  YouTube

Starting with Audio

Initially, the podcast offered audio-only content. Collaborating with our podcast host, Peter, I handle audio mixing using Audacity. This involves selecting appropriate background music, routine noise reduction, tempo adjustments, equalization (EQ), and loudness normalization for each episode.

Venturing into the Video World

Upon surpassing 100,000 plays, we made the strategic decision to enhance the listening experience through the addition of video content. Consequently, we expanded our presence to YouTube, effectively broadening our reach to a wider and more diverse audience.

Single Tone, Dual Styles

In response to the YouTube audience's evolving preferences, we introduced a new podcast series, Think Out Of The Box, in addition to our original, academically-focused series, Cowgor's Talk. The new series is characterized by its brevity and entertaining style, exploring unconventional and counterintuitive topics.

To distinguish between the two series, a distinctive visual theme was designed for the new series, enhancing its recognizability. I've retained a consistent tone with the original series to maintain a sense of continuity, ensuring that our audience can still connect with our content seamlessly.

CLTDC
Website Renovation

Elevating the Chinese Language Teaching Development Centre (CLTDC) website at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) with a fresh and contemporary redesign, enhancing a 20-year legacy.

View Website

A Modern Transformation

The website's style remained unchanged for more than a decade since its launch. While it remained functional, the user interface appears outdated, giving users an old-school impression that did not align with the innovative image of the center. A fresh and modern update provides a more user-friendly experience.

Responsive Design

Responsive Design ensures an optimal viewing experience by adapting the layout to different screen sizes. Implementing this approach on CLTDC's website guarantees accessibility and engagement, enhancing the overall usability. This not only aligns with modern web standards but also showcases CLTDC's commitment to digital innovation.

Defining a Brand.

Podcast
Brand Identity

Establish the podcast's brand identity by crafting a unified appearance through logo design, cover art, and social media templates.

  Instagram

Color of Milk

The podcast aims to simplify complex content, hosted by Mr. Cow and named "Cow Gor Talk". Inspired by the easy-to-digest nature of milk, the brand features a color palette derived from milk and old-fashioned milk bottles, with creamy white and flame scarlet as the primary colors. This blend creates a harmonious yet impactful impression on the audience.

Horizontal Logo

Full-size logo is excellent, but not always practical. A horizontal version enhances versatility while preserving the essence of the retro-style font, cow image, and signature color, ensuring adaptability in various design settings.

The Art of Cover

Using an irregular shape and a harmonious color palette as the cover art background, I aim to symbolize the podcast's approachable nature. The design ensures a perfect balance between information and aesthetics, with each episode's details seamlessly integrated into the graphic for a delightful visual experience.
